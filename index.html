Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms does SPARKLE support?

SPARKLE currently supports PC and Mac. Keep in mind that every product will have compatibility information in its specifications section.

Are the products eligible for a refund?

All of our games are eligible for a refund within a 14 days grace period. Please keep in mind that consumables are not eligible for refunds. Check the product's page and look for the tag refundable or non-refundable.

Which payment methods are supported?

SPARKLE supports payments through credit cards, cash(in-store) or PayPal.

Can i buy something as a gift?

Of course! You can buy gift cards or games as gifts, with special thematic packaging for physical game copies. For digital games, you just have to give us the recipient's SPARKLE account.
